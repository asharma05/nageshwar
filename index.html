NAGESHWAR

Nageshwar epitomizes the pinnacle of Marwari horse breeding, tracing his lineage to the prestigious bloodline of Aalishan. Standing tall at an impressive height of 65 inches, Nageshwar inherits his regal stature from his sire, the legendary stallion Sarang, and his great-grand sire, the revered Alishan Rohet Garh. His lineage is a testament to generations of excellence in Marwari horse breeding. With his striking presence and impeccable lineage, Nageshwar embodies the grace, strength, and beauty that define the Marwari breed.

As a stallion open for stud services, he offers breeders the opportunity to perpetuate these remarkable traits in future generations. Currently residing at D.C STUD Farm in Rampura Mahesana, Gujarat, India, Nageshwar stands ready to leave his mark on the world of Marwari horses.
